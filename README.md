# 🌾 AI Krishi Mechanic
### Voice-First, Safety-Aware Troubleshooting for Farmers

---

## 🚜 Problem

Agriculture still engages a significant portion of the population, yet farmers often lack immediate access to reliable mechanical support.

Farmers face:
- 🚫 Delayed or unavailable mechanics  
- 📖 Language barriers in manuals  
- 🔤 Low literacy levels  
- 🌐 Poor connectivity  
- ⚠️ Unsafe trial-and-error repairs  

Farmers describe issues in **symptoms**, not technical terms:

> "There’s a tapping sound."  
> "The tractor stops after 10 minutes."

Existing digital tools don’t match this natural communication style.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

**AI Krishi Mechanic** is a voice-first, local-language AI assistant that helps farmers troubleshoot agricultural equipment safely.

Instead of vague advice, it provides clear, structured outcomes:

- ✅ **Proceed**
- ⚠️ **Proceed with Caution**
- 🛑 **Escalate to Professional**

The system is intentionally designed to **stop and escalate when safety is uncertain.**

---

## 🧠 How It Works

1. 🎙 Farmer describes the issue in local language  
2. 📝 Speech → Text conversion  
3. 🔍 AI extracts symptoms & context  
4. ⚖ Risk + confidence assessment  
5. 📢 Structured safety decision  
6. 📞 Escalation to mechanic if needed  

---
